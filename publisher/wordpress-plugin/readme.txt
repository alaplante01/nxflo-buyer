== Description ==

Nexflo Ad Revenue Booster uses **header bidding** technology to make advertisers compete for your ad space in real time. More competition = higher prices = more revenue for you.

**Why Nexflo?**

* **One-click setup** — Install the plugin, enter your Site ID, done.
* **No ad-tech knowledge required** — We handle the complex stuff.
* **Auto-placement** — Ads are placed in optimal positions automatically.
* **Works with any theme** — Responsive ads that fit your layout.
* **Faster than AdSense** — Our server-side bidding doesn't slow your site down.
* **Free to use** — We only make money when you make money.

**How it works:**

1. Install the plugin
2. Sign up at [nexflo.ai](https://nexflo.ai/publishers/signup) to get your free Site ID
3. Enter your Site ID in Settings → Nexflo Ads
4. Start earning more from your ads

**Features:**

* Automatic ad placement (leaderboard, in-content, sidebar)
* Manual placement with shortcodes
* Mobile-responsive ad sizes
* Floor price control
* Customizable bid timeout
* Works alongside existing ads

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `nexflo-ads` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Settings → Nexflo Ads
4. Enter your Site ID (get one free at nexflo.ai/publishers/signup)
5. Save settings — ads will start showing immediately

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need an account? =

Yes, you need a free Site ID from [nexflo.ai](https://nexflo.ai/publishers/signup). It takes 30 seconds to sign up.

= Will this slow down my site? =

No. We use server-side header bidding, which means the auction happens on our servers, not in your visitor's browser. This is significantly faster than traditional client-side header bidding.

= Can I use this with Google AdSense? =

Yes. Nexflo works alongside AdSense. In many cases, the competition from header bidding will increase your overall ad revenue.

= What ad sizes are supported? =

All standard IAB sizes including 728x90 (leaderboard), 300x250 (rectangle), 320x50 (mobile banner), 160x600 (skyscraper), and more.

= How much can I earn? =

Revenue depends on your traffic, niche, and geography. Most publishers see a 20-50% increase over AdSense alone.

= Is my data safe? =

Yes. We are GDPR compliant and never sell your personal data. See our [privacy policy](https://nexflo.ai/privacy).
